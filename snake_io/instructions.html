How to Play

Welcome to Snake Lightning! Your goal is to survive as long as possible and get the highest score.

• Use your cursor or finger to move the snake.
• Collect glowing Orbs to grow longer and earn points.
• Pick up Lightning Bolts for a temporary speed boost.
• Hold the Space Bar to boost your speed at any time.
• While boosting with Space, your snake loses length over time.
• If your snake becomes too short, you can no longer boost.
• Use boosting strategically to reach Orbs quickly or escape danger.
• Avoid crashing into walls.
• Avoid running into your own tail.
• The game ends if you hit a wall or yourself.
• Try to collect as many Orbs as possible before the game is over.
• This is a single-player game, so challenge yourself to beat your best score!

Items

🟢 Orb = Grow longer + Score points
⚡ Lightning = Temporary speed boost

Controls

↑ ↓ ← → = Move Snake
Space Bar = Speed Boost (Costs Length)

Goal

Eat Orbs, survive as long as possible, manage your length wisely, and set a new high score!

Good luck, and have fun!
